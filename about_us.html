Who We Are

Lampmedia Production is a hub for quality and affordable Graphic Designs, Printing and Multimedia services serving emerging Nigeria market. Lampmedia production is a startup company that is involved in Graphic Design, Motion Graphics, Content Creation, Web Design, Hosting and Development, Photography and Cinematography, Printing and Production of complimentary cards, banners, flyers, jotters, souvenirs, office documents like receipts, letterheads and invoice etc. and also large format printing.

Our Vision

To become a one-stop shop for every graphic design, digital printing and multimedia services accessible both online and offline.

To become one of the strongest brand in printing, graphics design and multimedia production company in Nigeria

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer high quality graphic design, digital printing and multimedia services for individuals, small and large scale businesses, by consistently providing exceptional services at a fair price with reasonable margin.
